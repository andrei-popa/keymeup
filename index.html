What is Keymeup?

Keymeup is a key holder that gives you a reminder the moment you pick up the keys or put them back. You can schedule any important reminder that you need to see before leaving home or right after getting back.

Where's the magic?

The ideea behind Keymeup is to give you crucial reminders without disrupting your every day flow. No push buttons, no additional wearables, no extra actions. You just pick up your keys before going out the door.
